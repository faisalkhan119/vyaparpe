Risk & Mitigation

🛡️ Risks We've Already Solved

Every investor question — answered with a structured safety net

⚠️ Risk 1: Seller Fails on Ads

What if a managed seller's ads don't generate sales?

✅ Mitigation:
1. Ad commission (2%) is GUARANTEED — seller funds ads, we earn regardless.
2. Seller chooses: Option A (Full ₹5K refund & exit) or Option B (Forfeit ₹5K, get 5%/mo discount for 20 months on ₹1K+ plan).

⚠️ Risk 2: High Churn Rate

What if sellers leave after Month 1?

✅ Mitigation:
1. Option A builds massive market trust (zero-loss promise).
2. Option B locks seller into SaaS for 20 months.
3. Managed Plan = paid acquisition channel for SaaS.

⚠️ Risk 3: Low Sales Volume

What if sellers have low GMV?

✅ Mitigation:
1. 🚚 Courier margin covers 37% of costs alone.
2. Break-even at just ₹65K GMV/seller.
3. SaaS model is profitable in ALL 3 scenarios — even worst case.

🎯 Bottom Line: We win if sellers succeed (commissions + courier). We still win if they fail (ad commission guaranteed + SaaS conversion). There is no losing scenario for VyapaarPe.
